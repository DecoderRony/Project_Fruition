ABOUT THE SITE

Features of our site

This site is basically aimed to get students updated about their department's information shared by their CR. CR's can login using their own ID and Password and can update any kind of information they want to share. Student's will get a mail as the information gets updated and they can visit the site and check their particular departments information just on a single tap.
